FIELD EXPERIMENT

FINALISTS

The Goat Farm and Hambidge are thrilled to announce the 5 finalists for FIELD EXPERIMENT. Selected from 130 applications received from 44 cities, 21 states, and 5 countries including Germany, Canada, Spain, and the UK. The proposals included experimentations in the natural sciences & applied sciences, new media, movement, sound based work, transportation, architecture & design computation, music composition, participatory interventions, 2D & 3D visual art, large scale puppetry & materials engineering.
